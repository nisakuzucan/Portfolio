ME! ME! ME!!!

Grew up in Istanbul, Ankara and Connecticut, I spent most of my younger years yearning for memories and nostalgia, thank god I had art to keep me busy from becoming a 87 year old at the age of 7.

I love biking, drawing, discovering new places and imagining myself as a tiny bug going under rocks to throw secret bug parties and reading graphic novels.

I was an artist in my mother's womb, directing her like the rat in Ratatouille to draw and I always liked creating stuff whether it is in my mind or in physical realm, so let me help you create stuff! (could be in your mind or in physical realm)
